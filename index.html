Our Solution

The 3-Layer Shield System

BloomShield provides an AI-powered early warning system that integrates real-time Algae Tracking Sensors, predictive AI analytics, and autonomous drones into a unified platform.

1

Smart Trackers

Continuous water quality monitoring with solar-powered sensors tracking multiple parameters, including chlorophyll-a, phycocyanin, and dissolved oxygen.

2

Drone Surveillance

Automated drone deployment with multispectral imaging activated when risk exceeds critical levels, providing high-resolution visualization of water conditions.

3

AI Forecasting

Advanced machine learning predicts HAB risks up to 7 days in advance, with specific mitigation recommendations based on real-time data analysis.
